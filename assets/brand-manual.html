Color System — A. Digital System Palette

TECH PURPLE

HEX #6A1B9A RGB 106 / 27 / 154 CMYK 69 / 82 / 0 / 40

Usage

Tech Purple is the dominant brand identifier. Used for accent elements, CTA buttons, section markers, and key UI highlights. It is an accent driver, not a wallpaper.

This color feels technological, modern, and precise. It communicates SaaS energy and strong brand recognition without being playful or decorative.

Tech Purple #6A1B9A
System Black #111111
Dashboard Navy #1F2937
Grid Gray #F4F6F8
White #FFFFFF
Signal Green #16A34A

Digital Proportion

70% White
20% Black/Navy
8% Purple
2%

Purple is an accent driver, not a wallpaper. Signal Green is functional only — success states, active indicators. Never decorative. Kraft never appears in digital contexts.

Accessibility Contrast

White text on Purple — WCAG AA pass (7.2:1)
Purple text on White — WCAG AA pass (7.2:1)
White text on Navy — WCAG AA pass (12.6:1)
Green on dark backgrounds only — avoid on white
Never use purple text on navy — insufficient contrast
09
Color System — B. Material & Print Palette

INDUSTRIAL KRAFT

HEX #D6C2A1 RGB 214 / 194 / 161 CMYK 0 / 9 / 25 / 16 Tier 3 — Material Color Only

Usage

Kraft is used exclusively for physical materials to add material contrast. It never appears in digital systems.

It grounds the brand in the physical world — adding tactile depth to printed touchpoints without softening the tech identity.

Industrial Kraft #D6C2A1
Kraft Alt (aged) #C8B08A
Tech Purple (print) #6A1B9A

Print Proportion

60% White
20% Purple
15% Black
5% Kraft

Kraft Works In

  • ✓ Business cards (kraft stock)
  • ✓ Envelopes & mailers
  • ✓ Packaging & unboxing
  • ✓ Proposal covers & folders
  • ✓ Event materials & badges
  • ✓ Notebook / journal merch

Never Use Kraft In

  • × Dashboards or UI screens
  • × Mobile apps
  • × Digital presentations
  • × Social media templates
  • × Website backgrounds
  • × Email templates

Brand Architecture

Brand Structure

DOCEPROJECTS operates under a Branded House Model. All services and products sit under the master brand. No sub-brands. No alternative logos. No independent color systems.

DOCEPROJECTS
Automation
AI Systems
Dashboards
Bots
Process Engineering

All offerings inherit: logo, color system, typography, voice.

Naming Rules

Service naming must be clear, functional, and direct.

✓ DOCEPROJECTS Automation
✓ DOCEPROJECTS AI
✓ DOCEPROJECTS Dashboards
× FlowEngine
× AutoScale
× NeuroBot

No creative names. No abstract names. No metaphorical product names. The brand stands on execution, not clever naming.

  • × Hype language or startup clichés
  • × Passive voice or hedging
